About me

Seventeen years inside the rooms where careers are made.

I'm Maggie Wynn, founder of Magnolia Strategy Group. I spent most of my career leading talent acquisition and people strategy in tech — most recently as Head of Inclusion & Impact at Squarespace, and before that in senior recruiting roles there and at Dropbox. I'm known in my circles as a talent expert for Product and hiring.

I've sat on every side of the table: screening and hiring, coaching leaders, building equitable processes, and helping people through the messy, high-stakes moments of their working lives. What I love most is helping individuals move their careers forward and helping teams realize their ambitions.

My approach starts with empathy and leans on radical candor — I'll see the best in you and give you a realistic view of what's possible, then help you get there. My background is in sociology and women's studies, and that lens still shapes how I think about fairness and systems. I'm currently pursuing an ICF coaching credential to deepen the practice.
