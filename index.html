About me

Maggie Wynn, is the founder of Magnolia Strategy Group. She has spent almost 20 years building high performing teams in tech across everything from campus recruiting to senior executive and C-suite roles. She is considered an extension of the team she works with, as a strategic advisor to leaders on how to scale successfully, attract top talent, and intentionally build well-rounded teams. Most recently as the Global Head of Inclusion & Impact at Squarespace, she led strategic initiatives to empower, grow and support the workforce in their worldwide offices and built lasting programs and partnerships that aligned Squarespace values and business outcomes in the ESG space.

Having sat on every side of the table, Maggie is adept at screening and hiring, coaching leaders, building equitable processes, and helping people through the messy, high-stakes moments of their working lives. What she loves most is helping individuals move their careers forward and seeing them grow and thrive over years. She is not transactional, she’s in it with you for the long haul.

With an approach starting with empathy and grounded in reality — she sees the best in you and isn't afraid to deliver hard truths that will help you grow. She has a background in sociology and women's studies, which shapes how she thinks about fairness and systems. She is currently pursuing an ICF coaching credential to deepen the practice.
